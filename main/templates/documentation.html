Welcome to Atif Shop Market. Follow these steps to place your order successfully.

1. How to Place an Order

Select any product from our collection. You can either pay instantly using your Credit/Debit card through **Stripe** or choose the **WhatsApp/Local Transfer** method.

2. Local Payment (JazzCash/EasyPaisa)

If you choose Local Transfer, send the exact amount to our official number: 03075530176. Once the payment is sent, take a screenshot of the confirmation message.

3. Order Confirmation

Click on the **"Order via WhatsApp"** button or the Local Transfer box. Send us the product name and your payment screenshot. We will verify it and ship your order within 24 hours.
